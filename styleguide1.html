Fonts:

Georgia

Playfair Display

Open Sans

The reason why I chose these fonts is because they are simple and sophisticated which is the look I am going for this site. I styled the fonts in such a way that helps establish information hierachy on my pages. Playfair Display is used as a heading at the beginning of most pages, written in a big font size to indicate its importance and introduce the page to the user. Open Sans is a very standard font that is both readable and eligible; which is why I decided to make it the font for the main content of my pages and is stylized such as making it bold to highlight sub headings on some blog pages. Georgia is a slightly stylized font which is used for the figure caption on the design and data visulization page as well as for some sub headings for the blog pages to draw attention and point out importance. The site makes use of a combination of stylized and standard fonts the reason being that it creates a good design affect and makes it look charming. The consist use of fonts for certain aspects such as main content and headings helps to create balance throughout the site.

My site makes use of a variety of pastel colours, on the home page of my site the background picture is make up palette I made colour choices across the entire website to match the colours on the make up palette. Because the site is supposed to be a make up product store.I wanted to embody the essence of what makeup stands for which BEAUTY. The sites colours embody beauty and sophistication as well as being colourful in the most subtle way possible, the site itself looks like a colour palette which is the aim.
